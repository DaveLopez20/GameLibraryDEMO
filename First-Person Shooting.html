First Person Shooters are the most well-known genre in video games. The genre is mostly used in Survival and Military situations since, most of the time, the players would use guns. You end up seeing how guns and grenades are used, even fictional weaponry. They are mainstream because of the game's player base. The genre is mostly been played by adults, teenagers, and, unsurprisingly, kids. Games like that can bring a lot of memories for you and the people who play it.

Recommendations

For the new players, do you want to play a classic game with iconic multiplayer, an interesting campaign, and a great zombies mode. The game I'm talking about is Call of Duty Black Ops 2. Out of it series it's the most iconic since it has made many memories for people who played this game. It's multiplayer that has a lot of memorable maps including Nuketown 2025. The campaign was interesting since it was the first few games in the series that implemented choices that would make the game replayable. Plus, the zombies mode is a lot of fun with friends and seeing how long you guys survive.
