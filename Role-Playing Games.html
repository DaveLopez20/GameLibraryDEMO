The Role-Playing genre involves you as the player and experience the world you are in. This genre can sometimes be turn-based or open-world. The genre is mostly known to upgrade stuff. Like the crew, weapons, armor, and even the player itself. This genre is known for its stories and worldbuilding since whatever you do can affect the game's world. Sometimes, you actually care for your teammates, and you get to know more about the lore in the game because you are invested to see what happens next.

Recommendations

For people new to gaming, do you want to steal hearts and save the common man, then play Persona 5 Royale. You started out as a student who got framed for a crime he did not commit, and you stumbled upon a place called a Palace, which is located in a dimension called Mementos. You and your friends started a group called the Phantom Thieves, and you stole the hearts of the corrupted to see them change their hearts. The game has cool gameplay and a really good soundtrack.
