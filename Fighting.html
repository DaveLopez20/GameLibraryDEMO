The Fighting game genre is known to be the aggressiveness of the genres. It's mostly you, the player, versus another player or a bot. The genre can give you many memories, like a great victory or a sufferable defeat. The characters you play can vary to people, creatures, bots, and many more. Like the First-Person Shooter Genre, it has a lot of tournaments that people attend. Stuff like that can bring people together to fight or to spectate the fight.
